# This Week in Velmoria
## Update 2.0.6

Greetings, Seeker.
This week in Velmoria, we’re rolling out Update 2.0.6, introducing Prayers, Daily Tributes, changes to Anima and Reputation.

**Let’s get into it.**

## Patch Highlights
- **Tributes** — A brand-new daily reward system is live. Log in each day to receive challenges, boss hunts, and streak rewards scaled to your level.
- **New Spells** — Several new spells have been added across magic, ranged, and melee combat styles. Seek them out.
- **Bug Fixes** — Advancements no longer trap players at level 20, the Mob Journal is back in working order, and the level-up popup has been tamed.

## Update 2.0.2 – Changes
### General
- Grass and bushes can now spread upon being bonemealed.
- Updated Happy Ghast animations.
- Hotbar now has extra nature details.
- Added banners for all gods.
- Added several new spells to magic, ranged and melee combat styles.
- Removed the copper age backport till the end flashes are fixed.
- Added Tributes, a fully-featured daily reward system, every time you log in you'll be given challenges, boss hunts, and login streak rewards scaled to your player level.
- Player model no longer rotates, and is draggable in the profile screen.
- Added a new form of teleport spell, unlocked via quests.
- Adjusted the levels of some bosses.
- Added titles that read minecraft stats directly.
- Added more cosmetic, boss and item titles.

### Prayers
- Added the Prayer Book and Sigil; you can activate prayers and presets from these respectively.
- Added the Pool of Glory, where you can edit your prayers, pledge to a god and tithe to them.
- Right-click to open a 3-tab shrine menu:
    - **Pledge Tab**: View all 6 gods, pledge to one. Gods are locked behind advancements until you earn them. Locked gods appear greyed with a lock icon.
    - **Presets Tab**: Full prayer preset manager embedded in the shrine. Create, edit, and name up to 5 presets without needing to use the Prayer Book.
    - **Tithe Tab**: Sacrifice held anima items for reputation with your pledged god. Conversion rates range from 1 rep (Raw Anima) up to 200 rep (Pristine Anima Crystal).

### Edicts
- Discovering a new edict combination grants +150 Ithren reputation.
- 4 prayers are gated behind edict combos:
    - **Piety** → Endure + Pietra + Vitalis.
    - **Communion** → Anima + Soul + Unify.
    - **Turmoil** → Consume + Soul + Umbra.
    - **Eternal Torment** → Consume + Ignis + Mortem.

### Bug Fixes
- Fixed various advancements causing players to be stuck at level 20.
- Mob Journal now correctly works (Thanks Kev!)
- Level-up popups no longer trigger every time xp is gained.
- Removed flickering and dark patches on the player model in the profile screen.
- Anima drain no longer plays the orb pickup sound on every tick; now plays at most once every 6 seconds.

### Slimes SMP
- Disabled the following items:
    - Frost Bite Dragon Priest Staff
    - Frost Spike Dragon Priest Staff
